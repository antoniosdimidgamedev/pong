# How to Play

## Objective

Be the first player to score **5 points**. A point is scored when the ball passes your opponent's paddle and hits the wall behind them.

## Rules

- Each player controls a paddle on their side of the screen
- The ball bounces off the top and bottom walls
- The ball bounces off paddles when hit
- Missing the ball gives the opponent 1 point
- First to 5 points wins
- After a win, press any key to return to the menu

## Game Modes

### Player vs Player (Local)

Two players share one keyboard. Player 1 controls the left paddle, Player 2 controls the right paddle.

### Player vs CPU

Play against the computer. Choose from three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard). This mode supports saving and loading your progress.

### LAN Multiplayer

Play against another person on the same local network. One player hosts a server, the other joins. See [LAN Multiplayer](LAN-Multiplayer) for details.

## Tips

- **Stay centered** — you can reach both top and bottom bounces faster
- **Anticipate the ball** — watch the ball's direction, not just its position
- **CPU players** are predictable at close range; mix up your shots
- **Chat** during multiplayer by pressing `/` — communication wins games
